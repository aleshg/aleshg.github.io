Here our AVD blog journey begins.

It's funny to announce a new website on the new website. Well, here it is, in it's barebones template-y form since I am not a web programmer and as a bonus a terrible blog writer.

I have to thank you all for supporting this game! Your feedback was the push that this project needed, and I am glad I can work in such close loop to the players.
Making the prototype free was an excellent way to get even more feedback and to fuel interest. Creating a discord server was also a huge leap forward. I hope you all had a bit of fun trying out this game in it's early development phase.

In the future this website will be together with steam community main source of insight into the game's development. In other platforms I will post just the changelog and link it here. Discord was a bit odd to post with since it is not a blog oriented app, but chat oriented one.

Well, will see how it goes.
